A Week in the Life of a Browser »

This is a new study that will begin in December 2009, and recur periodically. It will run for a week each time, and collect data about the basic performance of Firefox — how often Firefox crashes or restarts, how many bookmarks it has, and so on. Find out more about this study here.

Test Pilot 0.3

Test Pilot 0.3 is a substantial rewrite. It's now based on the same technology as Jetpack, which will allow studies to be updated, and new studies to be added, without the need for you to download anything or restart Firefox.

Tab Open/Close Study »

New in Test Pilot 0.2 is a study to find out what users do after opening or closing a tab. Find out more about this study here.
